::Overview

The DC and Baltimore Perlmongers invite you to join us for a workshop at the Silver Spring Civic Building in Silver Spring, Maryland on Saturday, April 6, 2019. The workshop will have two tracks of speakers, discussing innovative, exciting, and fun real-world usage of Perl and related languages.

::Registration

Register via Eventbrite. Registration is $50, which goes 100% into the conference expenses (mainly the venue). Free admission is available for speakers, students, and the currently unemployed. We REALLY WANT you to come to this workshop, so just let us know if you need any special accommodations -- registration fee or otherwise!

Register Now

::Venue

This year we are hosting at the Silver Spring Civic Building, which is three blocks from the DC Metro transit system. Garage parking is also available and the venue is located near food and other attractions.

8525 Fenton Street, Silver Spring, MD 20910 (Google Maps, Apple Maps)

For more information and options on how to get there, check out our Venue page.>
