Regarding COVID-19

Due to vigilance around COVID-19, the organizers have decided to cancel the 2020 workshop and hackathon/community day. If you paid for your ticket, we will be refunding your purchase. We hope to see you at the next DCBPW, anticipated to be held in the Spring of 2021.

We are exploring other ways to celebrate the Perlyglot community this year, so please follow us on Twitter (@dcbpw) and check back here to stay up-to-date!

::Overview

The DC-Baltimore Perlyglot Workshop 2020 was a two-day technical conference in Baltimore, MD, which will be held on Saturday, April 18th and Sunday, April 19th. Saturday will feature two concurrent sessions of talks by volunteers, covering Perl and related technologies. Sunday will be an open code hacking day for learning, exploration, and collaboration for attendees to dig into everything tech has to offer.

::Sponsors

If you would like to help sponsor this workshop:

The DC-Baltimore Perlyglot Workshop is made possible by support from local business contributions and The Perl Foundation (TPF). Please take this opportunity to become a sponsor of the DCBPW-2020 event! If you aren't comfortable asking your company to make a donation but think we could be a good team, we'll do the asking! Send your sponsorship interest and potential sponsor contact information to dcbpw-organizers@googlegroups.com. Sponsorship is made in the form of a tax-deductible donation to The Perl Foundation, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.
