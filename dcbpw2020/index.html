::Overview

The DC-Baltimore Perl Workshop 2020 is a two-day technical conference in Baltimore, MD, which will be held on Saturday, April 18th and Sunday, April 19th. Saturday will feature two concurrent sessions of talks by volunteers, covering Perl and related technologies. Sunday will be an open code hacking day for learning, exploration, and collaboration for attendees to dig into everything tech has to offer.

::Venue

Johns Hopkins University*
Charles Commons
10 E. 33rd St
Baltimore, MD 21218

::Sponsors

If you would like to help sponsor this workshop:

The DC-Baltimore Perl Workshop is made possible by support from local business contributions and The Perl Foundation (TPF). Please take this opportunity to become a sponsor of the DCBPW-2020 event! If you aren't comfortable asking your company to make a donation but think we could be a good team, we'll do the asking! Send your sponsorship interest and potential sponsor contact information to dcbpw-organizers@googlegroups.com. Sponsorship is made in the form of a tax-deductible donation to The Perl Foundation, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.

2019-09-20 Save the date!

*Drum roll, please* ... We have selected The Johns Hopkins University* in Baltimore, Maryland as our 2020 workshop venue.

Save the date! The DC-Baltimore Perl Workshop will be held on Saturday, April 18, 2020 and an open code hacking day will follow on Sunday, April 19, 2020.

2019-07-22 The 2020 DCBPW is happening!

Hello again! This is just a quick notice that, yes, we're on target for a 2020 DCBPW in early april in the Baltimore area. At just an early stage, we have not yet absolutely decided on the venue, and we'll do that before we start recommending and/or making arrangements with the nearest hotels. Still, we wanted to let you know. Check back here for updates.
