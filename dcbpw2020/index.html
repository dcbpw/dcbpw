::Call for Talk Proposals!!

Proposals usually cover a wide range of topics like regular expressions, hashes and arrays, unicode, making forms pretty, all about my favorite JavaScript library, managing a group of mongers, parsing logs, my favorite template engine, getting started with OO programming, OO Programming with Module::Name and more.

This workshop is what we call perlyglot, given our beloved roots in perl, but affection for other languages and related technologies as well. Don't immediate discount a presentation idea simply because it may not be based on perl!

All level talks are welcome, beginner to advanced. You don't need to have a finalized presentation ready when you submit a talk proposal - just fill out the form with your topic and summary!

Deadline is January 6, 2020!!

::Overview

The DC-Baltimore Perlyglot Workshop 2020 is a two-day technical conference in Baltimore, MD, which will be held on Saturday, April 18th and Sunday, April 19th. Saturday will feature two concurrent sessions of talks by volunteers, covering Perl and related technologies. Sunday will be an open code hacking day for learning, exploration, and collaboration for attendees to dig into everything tech has to offer.

::Registration

Registration is $50, which goes 100% into the conference expenses (mainly the venue). Free admission is available for speakers, students, and the currently unemployed. We REALLY WANT you to come to this workshop, so just let us know if you need any special accommodations — registration fee or otherwise!

The DC and Baltimore Perl Mongers are committed to creating a welcoming and harassment-free environment, and all attendees and speakers must agree and adhere to our standards of conduct. Please click on the button below to read our policy.
